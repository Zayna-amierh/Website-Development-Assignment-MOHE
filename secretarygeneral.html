Secretary General

Professor Ahed AL Wahadni

Higher education has witnessed substantial development over the last decade in terms of the high quality of education and different academic programs in Jordanian higher education institutions. The transformative growth in using modern teaching methods and technologies has attracted a large number of Arab and foreign students from different parts of the world to choose Jordan as a destination for study.

However, There is still an extensive scope for expansion and evolution and we will spare no efforts to maximize the favourable conditions and continuous growth of the higher education sector.

Finally, on behalf of all the staff of the Ministry of Higher Education & Scientific Research, I welcome you to our website. As you browse through our website, I hope you will sense our commitment to provide the level of service you expect and deserve. We are here to assist you and certainly encourage your feedback regarding any issues.
